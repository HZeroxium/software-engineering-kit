## Validation Criteria

- Activates only when relevant.
- Produces expected output structure.
- Does not conflict with global rules.
- Does not invent commands or APIs.
- Preserves safety boundaries.
- Improves repeatability.
- Does not add unnecessary context overhead.

## Rollback / Disable

- Remove or rename the artifact.
- Narrow the description.
- Move detailed content into supporting files.
- Deprecate or merge with overlapping artifact.
- Keep backup if needed.

## Audit Point

- After first 3 uses.
- After repeated AI failure.
- After tool behavior changes.
- After workflow changes.
