## Acceptance Criteria Quality Check

Each criterion should be:

- Observable.
- Testable.
- Unambiguous.
- Independent where possible.
- Grounded in confirmed requirements.
- Clear about user-visible behavior.
- Clear about error behavior.
- Clear about permission behavior when relevant.
