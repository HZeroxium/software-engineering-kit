# Governance Risk Checklist

- [ ] Owner identified or marked unknown.
- [ ] Data owner identified or marked unknown.
- [ ] Architecture decision record considered.
- [ ] Policy enforcement point identified.
- [ ] Auditability considered.
- [ ] Vendor/third-party risk considered.
- [ ] Cost owner considered.
- [ ] Manual approval gates identified.
- [ ] Compliance claims avoided unless evidence exists.
- [ ] Risk acceptance owner identified if risk remains.
